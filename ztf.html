ZTF Meeting Schedule

Here is the meeting schedule for the different science working groups in ZTF. Time zone is Pacific.

  • Multi-Messenger Astrophysics (EM-GW, EM-neutrino): Tues. 11am, every 2 weeks (from Nov 21)
  • Supernovae and Relativistic Explosions: Wed. 10am
  • AGN and TDEs: Wed. 2:30pm
  • Cosmology with SNe and gravitational lensing: Thurs. 7:30am, every 2 weeks (from Nov 9)
  • Solar system bodies: 2nd and 4th Monday of the month at 4:30pm
  • Stellar science: 1st and 3rd Tuesday of the month at 4:30pm, 2nd and 4th Tuesday at 10am, 5th Tuesday no telecon
  • M31: TBD

Superbias Frames

Here is a tool to visualize superbias frames from the month of November. For a given night, each superbias frame was constructed as follows:

  • Each bias frame was median-subtracted
  • All bias frames were median-combined
The preliminary median-subtraction was done in order to remove time-dependent variation and leave only spatial variation.
