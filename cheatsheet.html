Trigger the VLA for a transient

  • Log onto my.nrao.edu and enter the obs prep tool
  • Look up a flux calibrator. Note that 3C295 should not be used above L-band in D- and C-configurations.
  • Look up a phase calibrator
  • In the scans, the flux calibrator is used to flux density and bandpass calibration
  • The setup intent should be at the position of the first source you observe -- the idea is that this gives you time to get there
  • Validation and submission: click through time windows and check that you don't encounter any errors
